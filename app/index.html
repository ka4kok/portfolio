About me

Who i am

Hello, my name is Igor Ivanov. I am a front-end developer. My interest in the development of attractive sites is what made me focus on the development of interfaces. My goal is to start a career junior front-end developer and grow to a team-lead.

WHAT I DO

I have a big passion for creating useful and beautiful things, while constantly trying to learn and improve my skills in the process. I offer front-end solutions, making using of cutting edge technologies. I like desktop-first responsive design, progressive enhancement and single page applications.

NEAT FACTS

Coding: Apart from HTML, CSS and JS; Version control systems: Git; project collector: Gulp; Preprocessors: SCSS, SASS, Handlebars

Education: Belarusian State University, Physicians Faculty

Hobbies: I play basketball, do athletics, I like to walk and listen to music

Languages: I speak and write Russian, Belarus and read on English!
